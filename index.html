About Ashley

Ashley M. Bettencourt-Pimenta is a graphic designer based in Atlanta, Georgia, specializing in brand identity, print campaigns, and digital design. She holds a BFA in Graphic Design, Advertising and Branding from the Savannah College of Art and Design, graduating cum laude and honored on the Dean's List.

Her client work spans entertainment, technology, and sports. She spent several years at Warner Bros. Discovery's CNN Creative Marketing division, producing campaigns for programming including Stanley Tucci: Searching for Italy, CNN Election Night 2024, and Breaking Bread. Prior to CNN, she created promotional materials for TBS and TNT networks including Team Coco, Search Party, and Miracle Workers.

Her work has been recognized with a Digital Distinction Award (2020) and an SCAD AAF ADDY's nomination (2019), and has appeared in exhibitions at Atlantic Station, Madison Yards, and Cultured South.
