  BOOK DESIGN
  The objective was to create a book on any topic. I chose my favorite artist, Jean‑Michel Basquiat, a young creative force that emerged from New York City. Basquiat’s complex upbringing exposed him to a variety of different cultures, which influenced him to adapt distinctive elements of Non-Western art that introduced a new type of aesthetic of Modernism in the 1970s.

  The Art and Life of Basquiat encompasses the journey of a passionate painter through the early, middle, and late stages of his artistic career. Containing three chapters in chronological order, each delve into his works during that time including Basquiat’s sketchings printed on fine toothed drawing paper and paintings printed on coated paper.
