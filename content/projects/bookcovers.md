  BOOK DESIGN
  Mental illnesses are often overlooked and misunderstood. Given this, the objective of this project was to spread awareness and bring light to these unfortunate issues. This book series explores the depth of mental disorders, focusing on anxiety, chronic depression, and bipolar disorder.

  Those who have mental disorders are more likely to experience magnified emotions, alterations of perception, and moments of skewed judgment. This was achieved through vibrant visuals, intriguing patterns and textures that are visually engaging.
