  GRAPHIC DESIGN | ART DIRECTION
  We all have secrets, but should the government have theirs? The government denies all knowledge of any possible existence of extraterrestrial life and its presence on Earth. The objective was to ultimately spread the truth of alien existence and to expose the government of its corrupt secrets. Finally, there is proof and the public deserves to know.

  This was accomplished through multiple deliverables that primarily spun off traditional 1960-70s visuals, some with a modern twist, and mimeographic techniques. Given that this project was created for that era, all deliverables are print based and in the perspective of a group we created called "Underground Press" releasing all of these confidential documents.
